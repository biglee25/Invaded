Our Process

Over the years, we have adopted many processes to adapt to current design trends.

We have developed a bespoke set of design processes that is proven to get results and deliver stunning designs and websites that our clients require.

Our Brand Process

Brand Discovery

First, the target audience needs to be identified. Defining the target audience is so important because we need to design strategically for the best outcome. As well as collecting design and business information here, we collect links to different brands that they love, competitors in their industry and brands that they like and dislike.

Design Concepts

We now start creating concepts for the logo. we always begin on paper, sketching out ideas and getting creative. we then take ideas some of the best ideas and create presentable digital logos We will then provide the client with at least 3 unique designs to choose from.

Hi-Fidelty Design

Once a design is agrred upon, a high resolution, high fidelty design is created. This is where the design comes to life. Once presented to the client, final alterations are considered to really fine tune the design.

Final Presentation

Once a design is agreed upon, a high resolution, high fidelty design is created. This is where the design comes to life. Once presented to the client, final alterations are considered to really fine tune the design.

Design Hand-off

With all the alerations made and client is happy with the final submission, the finished design is packaged and sent to the client in .ai .eps and .jpg formats.

Our Website Process

Web Discovery

It's important to find out at this stage what the client requires from the outcome of the website to gain an understanding as to what direction and technology stack to implement. Detailed notes are taken from the client in order to make a decision on this. This is the stage where any extra services you require will be made available to you, whether it is on-going website maintenance or content creation.

Website Mockup

From the information taken from the client, a asic mockup or prototype is created for the sole purpose of giving the client an idea of the design and functionality of the website. the client will then have time to make suggestions and changes in order to move forward. Please note: this stage is very important and only minor, cosmetic changes will be allowed after this stage has been signed off.

Website Design

Once everything is agreed with the client, work will begin on creating the website based upon the agreed prototype that the client has chosen. The website will be developed on a staging platform and the client will have full input as to the direction and changes being made.

Website Sign-off

When the finished website is complete, the client will have access to the staging platform ready to sign off on the comleted website. At this stage, any web hosting or domain names already owned, should be made available to us.

Website Launch

Once the client is happy with the finished website, arrangements will be made to attach domain names and hosting to complete the process. Final checks are made by us that everything is working as intended and that the website is responsive on all leading browsers. Once complete, the website is launched and the work is complete.
